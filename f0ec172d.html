Normal Distribution

$$ f(x|\mu,\tau) = \sqrt{\frac{\tau}{2\pi}} exp(-\frac{\tau}{2} (x - \mu)^2) $$

A distribution of real numbers centered on a mean \(\mu\), spread out according to the precision \(\tau\).

Properties

  • expected value: \(E[Z|\mu,\tau] = \mu\)
  • variance: \(Var(Z|\mu,\tau) = \frac{1}{\tau}\)
