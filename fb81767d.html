Exponential Distribution

$$ f_Z(z|\lambda)=\lambda e^{-\lambda z}, z \geq 0 $$

Exponential random variables can take on any non-negative value.

Properties

  • The expected value of an exponential random variable is the inverse of \(\lambda\): \(E[Z|\lambda]=\frac{1}{\lambda}\)
